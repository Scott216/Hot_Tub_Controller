This sketch controls the hot tub control panel.  On the panel is an LCD display, 
three momentary pushbuttons that can be illuminated and a rotary encoder with a built-in
pushbutton.

The Arduino is a pro-mini 3.3 volts and communicates with the main control board via I2C.
